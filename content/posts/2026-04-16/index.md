We are pleased to announce DGrid’s partnership with Stable. Through this collaboration, DGrid will provide AI infrastructure to the Stable ecosystem, supporting the integration and invocation of large language models across a growing range of ecosystem AI use cases. As Stable builds USDT-native infrastructure designed for real-world payments, seamless user experience, and scalable adoption, DGrid is proud to support the ecosystem from the AI infrastructure layer.

## Supporting an Ecosystem Built for Practical Adoption

Stable’s focus on real-world usability, efficient settlement, developer accessibility, and scalable performance makes it a strong foundation for ecosystem AI.

From DGrid’s perspective, as ecosystems become more application-driven, AI must move beyond experimentation and into real products and workflows. That makes infrastructure essential to helping AI integrate more naturally into practical use cases.

## Why AI Infrastructure Matters

At DGrid, we believe the next stage of ecosystem AI growth depends on how efficiently intelligence can be connected to real demand.

AI infrastructure helps lower integration barriers, improve model accessibility, and support more reliable invocation across use cases. In an ecosystem like Stable, where usability and scalability are core priorities, this foundation becomes even more important.

## Contributing to the Long-Term Growth of Ecosystem AI

We see this partnership as part of a broader shift in which AI and digital infrastructure are becoming more closely connected.

As Stable continues building rails for fast, predictable, and developer-friendly stablecoin activity, DGrid aims to contribute to the AI infrastructure that helps intelligent applications grow more smoothly across the ecosystem.

Looking ahead, DGrid is excited to support the continued growth of AI within the Stable ecosystem.

We believe there is strong potential for AI to become a more natural part of Stable’s ecosystem evolution, and DGrid is proud to help support that foundation.

## About Stable

Stable is a Layer 1 blockchain designed for seamless financial transactions, powered by USDT as the native gas token. Its design prioritizes usability, reducing friction for end users by eliminating the need for volatile tokens for transaction fees. Ideal for peer-to-peer payments, remittances, and cross-border transactions, Stable enables virtually instant settlement, enhances capital efficiency, and creates a user-friendly ecosystem for developers and users alike.
