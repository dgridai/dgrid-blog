We’re excited to announce our partnership with SentismAI.

SentismAI is building a new generation of on-chain applications, spanning AI-driven investing, intelligent DeFi workflows, and real-world asset management. DGrid is joining that vision from the infrastructure side, supporting the integration and invocation of large language models across the SentismAI ecosystem.

This partnership brings together two complementary layers: SentismAI’s application-facing ecosystem and DGrid’s AI infrastructure. One is focused on how AI is experienced in products; the other is focused on how AI is connected, accessed, and put to work behind the scenes.

## Where the Collaboration Connects

What makes this collaboration meaningful is the fit between both sides.

SentismAI is creating products where AI is not just a feature, but part of the product flow itself, whether through OneRouter, LaunchOn, Smart Account, or its mini app ecosystem. These are environments where users are not only consuming information, but interacting, deciding, deploying, and executing on-chain.

DGrid supports that kind of ecosystem by making it easier to connect applications with model intelligence. As AI becomes more embedded in real product behavior, the infrastructure layer matters more: not only for access to models, but for how smoothly intelligence can move into user-facing workflows.

## Mutual Support Across the Stack

This collaboration reflects a shared direction rather than a one-sided integration.

SentismAI brings real usage scenarios, product surfaces, and on-chain demand that give AI practical ground to operate in. DGrid contributes the infrastructure layer that helps support those experiences with stronger model integration and invocation.

In that sense, both sides reinforce each other. SentismAI creates more room for AI to be applied in real Web3 environments, while DGrid helps make those AI capabilities more usable, responsive, and deployable across a growing product ecosystem.

## Why This Matters

The next phase of AI in Web3 will likely be shaped less by isolated model capability and more by how well intelligence connects with products that people actually use.

That is why this collaboration matters. It sits at the intersection of intelligence and execution: models, data, workflows, user actions, and on-chain outcomes. When infrastructure and applications evolve together, AI moves closer to becoming a practical layer of Web3 rather than a separate experimental track.

## Looking Ahead

Looking ahead, we see strong potential in building this next phase together.

As SentismAI continues expanding AI-powered on-chain applications, DGrid will support the infrastructure side of that growth. Together, we hope to help shape a more usable path for AI in Web3, where intelligent systems are not only available, but increasingly connected to real products, real workflows, and real outcomes.
