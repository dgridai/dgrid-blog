We are pleased to announce DGrid’s partnership with Pieverse.

Through this collaboration, DGrid will contribute AI infrastructure to the Pieverse ecosystem, supporting large language model integration and invocation across autonomous agent use cases. The partnership is focused on helping Pieverse strengthen the intelligence layer behind agent interaction, execution, and onchain utility.

## Supporting an Ecosystem Built for Agentic Use Cases

Pieverse is building infrastructure for autonomous AI agents in Web3, with products and protocols designed around agent-driven finance and commerce.

That direction is especially meaningful as AI agents begin moving beyond content and conversation into action. In ecosystems where agents are expected to interact, execute, and transact, model access alone is not enough. What matters more is whether intelligence can be integrated into products in a way that is usable, responsive, and adaptable to real scenarios.

## Why AI Infrastructure Matters

At DGrid, we believe the next stage of ecosystem AI growth depends on how efficiently intelligence can be connected to real use cases.

AI infrastructure helps reduce integration friction, improve model accessibility, and support more reliable invocation across different scenarios. In an ecosystem like Pieverse, where agent capabilities are closely tied to execution, interaction, and commerce, this foundation becomes especially important for enabling more practical AI adoption.

## Contributing to the Long-Term Growth of Ecosystem AI

At DGrid, we believe AI infrastructure becomes more important as agent systems become more operational.

For agent ecosystems, the challenge is not only calling a model, but supporting consistent invocation, smoother integration, and a more efficient path from intelligence to execution. This is particularly relevant in environments like Pieverse, where agents are being positioned closer to financial behavior, commerce flows, and user-facing interaction.

By supporting this layer, infrastructure can help reduce development friction, improve how model capabilities are applied, and make it easier for ecosystems to extend AI into more practical use cases.

Looking Ahead

Looking ahead, DGrid is excited to support the continued growth of AI within the Pieverse ecosystem.

We believe this collaboration can help strengthen the foundation for more capable autonomous agents and open the way for broader AI-driven innovation across Web3 finance and commerce.
