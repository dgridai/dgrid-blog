We are pleased to announce DGrid’s partnership with AltLLM.

AltLLM is building a crypto-native AI suite with products such as AltLLM Chat and AltClaw, bringing AI closer to real Web3 use cases through built-in crypto intelligence and autonomous agent infrastructure. Through this collaboration, both teams will work more closely across the infrastructure layer while supporting broader ecosystem growth together.

## A Shared Direction for Crypto AI

AltLLM is focused on making AI more usable in crypto, whether through Web3-aware chat experiences or autonomous agents that can operate across on-chain environments. DGrid shares that direction from the infrastructure side, with a focus on helping AI systems become more efficient, reliable, and adaptable as crypto-native products continue to evolve.

This creates a collaboration built not only around shared vision, but also around strengthening some of the underlying foundations that support better product performance and broader adoption.

## Working Together on Infrastructure and Growth

As both teams continue building for crypto-native AI, this partnership will focus on closer coordination around infrastructure, ecosystem collaboration, and market expansion.

From DGrid’s perspective, part of that role is to help reinforce the technical layer behind crypto AI experiences as they scale across more products, users, and use cases. At the same time, both sides see value in working together beyond the technical stack, especially in expanding ecosystem reach and helping bring stronger visibility to this emerging category.

## Looking Ahead

Crypto AI is entering a stage where products, infrastructure, and distribution are increasingly shaping each other.

As that happens, progress depends not only on what AI products can do, but also on the strength of the systems supporting them behind the scenes. We believe collaborations like this can help create a more solid foundation for crypto-native AI to grow in a more practical, scalable, and connected way.

Looking ahead, DGrid is excited to work alongside AltLLM in supporting the next phase of crypto AI development. We believe stronger coordination across infrastructure and ecosystem growth can help open new possibilities for crypto-native AI products, and we look forward to contributing to that momentum together.
